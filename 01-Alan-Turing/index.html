Alan Turing

Alan Turing, is a famous mathematician known for breaking the german nazi encryption code during World War II. He is also known as the father of the modern computer.

Some of Alan Turing's most notatalbe accomplishments

  • Bombe - The bombe, with an enhancement suggested by mathematician Gordon Welchman, became one of the primary tools, and the major automated one, used to attack Enigma-enciphered messages.
  • Delilah - Following his work at Bell Labs in the US,[83] Turing pursued the idea of electronic enciphering of speech in the telephone system.
  • Turingery - for use against the Lorenz cipher messages produced by the Germans' new Geheimschreiber (secret writer) machine.
